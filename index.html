About Our Project !

It is a smart farming stick based on IoT(Internet of things) technology which has brought revolution to each and every field of common man’s life by making everything smart and intelligent. Aim of this project is to propose a novel smart IoT based agriculture stick assisting farmers in getting live data(Temperature, soil moisture, smoke detection) for efficient environment monitoring which will enable them to do smart farming and increase their overall yield and quality of products

Conclusion!

In this project, a Smart Farming Enabled IoT Based Smart Farming Stick for monitoring of Water content and Temperature has been proposed using Sensor and LCD Display. The stick has high efficiency and fetching the live data of Water content and Temperature. The Farming stick being via this project will assist farmers in increasing the agriculture yield as the stick will always provide helping hand to farmers for getting accurate live feed of environmental temperature and soil moisture.
